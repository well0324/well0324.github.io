Q
What is the NTUILO? Is there a shorter and easier way to refer to it?
A

The National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office or NTUILO is one of the international industry-academia liaison bodies subsidized by the Ministry of Science and Technology. It is currently Taiwan’s only integrated, cross-sector and multi-themed industry-academia liaison organization.

The purpose of its establishment is to a mutual benefitting bridge between the industry and National Taiwan University, integrating National Taiwan University’s R&D momentum and providing membership enterprises with National Taiwan University’s cross-field and cross-industry R&D technology and talents.

Q
How are you different to other international industry-academia liaison offices, Industry-University Cooperative Research Project on Advanced Technology (Major Alliance) & Academia Industry Alliance for E-book?
A

Currently there are 15 universities that have accepted the international industry-academia liaison subsidy program from the Ministry of Science and Technology. With individual academic institution as a single unit, designated service contacts for the communication between schools and enterprises have been established. Since each academic institution has their own specialized focus and area of expertise, the services and resources offered by institutions will vary.

National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office, Industry-University Cooperative Research Project on Advanced Technology (Major Alliance) and Academia Industry Alliance for E-book are all important industry-academia cooperation programs promoted by the Ministry of Science and Technology, varying only in scope and goals. The National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office is a service unit that integrates large scale cross-department, cross-sector, cross-industry and cross-country industry-academia co-operations.

Q
What can National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office do for businesses?
A

The National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office adopts an enterprise membership system. Depending on the service needs, membership can be classified into: Elite, Superior and Laureates level memberships. Its main services include the matching of membership enterprises with institute teams and acts as the service contact for communication. Matching services include: Holding industry-academia events, project coordination, Taida Talent Hub, patent and research plan database etc.

Through National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office, enterprises can significantly reduce the arrangement costs when cooperating with National Taiwan University. It also provides enterprises with multi-theme and forms of cooperation and greatly enhances partnerships. The goal is to use institute R&D momentum to reignite enterprise growth, providing enterprises with new growth and prosperity for industry-academia co-operations.

Q
What are the differences between the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office Membership levels?
A

The Office offers Elite, Superior and Laureate level memberships. Based on the service content and items, the annual membership fees shall vary.

In terms of choosing membership level, we recommend enterprises that are yet to be affirmed of their goals and plans in cooperating with National Taiwan University, to choose Elite Level Membership. The year-long services will focus on the introduction, matching of professional teams and resources as well as planning services for possible industry-academia co-operation items.

Enterprises that are already familiar with the talents and research resources of National Taiwan University, or that already have plans to co-operate with National Taiwan University, are recommended to choose the Superior level membership. The year-long services will focus on deeper coordination and adjustment to existing plans as well as execute mature plants.

If enterprises have a strong cooperation foundation with National Taiwan University and understand the resources and advantages of involved, then the Laureate level membership is your best choice. Laureate members enjoy year-long, highly customizable liaison office services. The liaison office integrates National Taiwan University’s talent teams and resources, providing Laureate level enterprises with the most comprehensive, diverse cooperation programs, making the two the best of partnership buddies.

For more information on the different levels of memberships, please refer to “membership level description”

Q
Do all future co-operations with National Taiwan University have to go through the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office?
A

The purpose of the establishment of the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office was to create a bridge between enterprises and National Taiwan University, enhance the co-operation procedures and overall elevation of co-operation experiences. Thus, enterprises that have previous worked with National Taiwan University offices or its research teams, do not require the intervention of the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office to continue previous agreed items of co-operation. That being said, the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office plays the role of enhancing communications and not only does it serve as a communication window but also as the contact centre. If membership enterprises wish to expand co-operation, promote cross-sector industry-academia co-operation or plan new forms of cross-sector industry-academia, the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office the best partner.

Q
What can National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office do for the academic institute?
A

The core values of National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office are to connect industry, government and academic resources and promote a positive and benefiting cycle of industry, government, academic co-operation. It is an industry-academic co-operation exchange platform that integrates resources and promotes the formation of a national ecosystem that cultivates talent and industry transformation.

The National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Offices provides National Taiwan University administrative offices, college & departments, research institutes and student clubs with different forms of assistance and resources, such as consulting for start-up programs or industry sponsored partnership arrangements. All these and more can be provided by the National Taiwan University Industry Liaison Office
